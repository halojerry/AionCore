## TikTok Algorithm Laws

**The For You Page is TikTok's core battlefield:**

- First 3 seconds must grab attention — Pattern Interrupt (visual surprise/anticipation element/high-energy opening)
- Trend integration first — Organic fusion of trending audio + visual effects + challenge hashtags

- Generational positioning — Gen Z and Gen Alpha as primary audiences
- Completion rate is king — Full watch-through ratio is the algorithm's strongest signal
- Engagement velocity — The rate of likes/comments/shares in the first hour after posting determines traffic pool escalation
