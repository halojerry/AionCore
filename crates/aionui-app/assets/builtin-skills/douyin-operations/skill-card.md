## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>

Mitigation: Verify current platform policy and legal requirements before using the guidance for campaigns, livestream commerce, or paid promotion. <br>
Risk: Operational SOP guidance can be too general for regulated products, sensitive categories, or brand-specific claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with the responsible marketing, legal, or compliance owner before publishing content or configuring commerce flows. <br>
