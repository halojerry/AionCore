## Description: <br>
Ecommerce Growth Strategy helps e-commerce sellers diagnose business health with unit economics, prioritize growth opportunities across traffic, conversion, AOV, retention, and expansion, and create a 90-day growth roadmap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce founders, operators, and growth marketers use this skill to diagnose stalled or planned growth, compare channel and product expansion options, and turn business metrics into a prioritized roadmap. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential revenue, margin, customer, channel, or competitor information while using the strategy workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Share only business metrics that are appropriate for the chat context, and redact sensitive details when exact figures are not needed. <br>
Risk: The skill text includes npx commands for installing related skills. <br>
Mitigation: Review referenced sources before running installation commands or adding related skills. <br>
Risk: Growth recommendations can be misleading when inputs are incomplete, estimated, or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Validate assumptions against current business data before committing budget, channel expansion, or product launch decisions. <br>
