## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs, founders, and startup advisors use this skill to plan investor-facing pitch decks for seed, Series A, and other fundraising contexts. It helps tailor deck structure, narrative, slide content, visual guidance, and presentation preparation to the project type and target investor audience. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include confidential business, customer, or financial details while drafting a fundraising deck. <br>
Mitigation: Use only trusted tools for sensitive information and review generated content before sharing it externally. <br>
Risk: The skill may suggest using a separate pptx-generation skill, whose behavior is outside this release. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve any companion pptx-generation skill independently before installation or execution. <br>
Risk: Pitch decks can contain unsupported market, traction, technical, or financial claims. <br>
Mitigation: Require source-backed data, clear assumptions, and founder review before sending materials to investors. <br>
